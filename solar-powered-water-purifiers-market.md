<p><p>The solar-powered water purifiers market comprises various types, including Ultra-Filtration Systems and Reverse Osmosis (RO) Filtration Systems, among others. Ultra-Filtration Systems utilize membrane technology to eliminate particles, bacteria, and larger viruses while allowing water to retain essential minerals. RO Filtration Systems employ a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities at a molecular level, offering high purification rates for contaminated water. Other systems may include solar distillation or chemical treatment methods, catering to diverse water quality needs and user preferences.</p></p>
