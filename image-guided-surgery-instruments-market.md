<p><p>The Image Guided Surgery Instruments market comprises different imaging modalities used to enhance surgical precision. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images for accurate navigation. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) offers soft tissue contrast, aiding in complex surgeries. X-Ray Fluoroscopy delivers real-time imaging for dynamic procedures. Endoscopes visualize internal structures during minimally invasive surgeries. Position Emission Tomography (PET) assesses metabolic activity, while Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) offers 3D images of functional processes, supporting targeted treatments and interventions.</p></p>
