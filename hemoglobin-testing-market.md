<p><strong>The Hemoglobin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chromatography</li><li>Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemoglobin testing market is primarily categorized into two types: chromatography and immunoassay. Chromatography involves separating and analyzing the different forms of hemoglobin in blood samples, providing precise quantification of variants like hemoglobin A1c and fetal hemoglobin. Immunoassays, on the other hand, utilize antibodies to detect and measure hemoglobin levels, offering rapid results and ease of use in point-of-care settings. Both technologies cater to the needs of clinical diagnostics and research, enhancing patient care and disease management.</p></p>
