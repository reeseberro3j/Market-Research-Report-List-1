<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion</li><li>Direct Energy Deposition</li><li>Binder Jetting</li><li>Bound Powder Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal 3D Printing Service market features several key technologies: </p><p>1. **Powder Bed Fusion** utilizes a laser or electron beam to fuse metal powder layers, known for high precision and complexity in parts. </p><p>2. **Direct Energy Deposition** delivers focused energy to melt metal feedstock, ideal for repairs and adding features to existing parts. </p><p>3. **Binder Jetting** involves applying a binder to metal powder layers, creating a solid part after sintering. </p><p>4. **Bound Powder Extrusion** combines metal powder with a polymer binder, allowing extrusion into complex shapes before removing the binder.</p></p>
