<p><p>The blood glucose test strip market is primarily segmented into two types: Glucose Oxidase and Glucose Dehydrogenase. Glucose Oxidase strips utilize an enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce an electrochemical signal, commonly found in traditional monitoring devices. In contrast, Glucose Dehydrogenase strips employ a different enzymatic reaction that allows for improved accuracy and minimal interference from other sugars. Both types serve the critical role of aiding diabetes management, ensuring effective glucose level monitoring for patients.</p></p>
