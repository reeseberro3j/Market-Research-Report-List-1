<p><p>The sealed gauge pressure sensor market is categorized into three types based on pressure ranges: low pressure (typically below 2 bar), mid pressure (ranging from 2 to 40 bar), and high pressure (above 40 bar). Low-pressure sensors are used in applications like HVAC systems, while mid-pressure sensors serve industries such as manufacturing and process control. High-pressure sensors are designed for demanding environments such as oil and gas exploration, requiring robust construction to withstand extreme conditions and deliver accurate measurements.</p></p>
