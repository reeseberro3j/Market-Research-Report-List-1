<p><p>Reporter gene assay kits are utilized in molecular biology to study gene expression through various mechanisms. Luciferase assay kits measure bioluminescence, providing high sensitivity for detecting gene activity. Fluorescent protein-related kits utilize fluorescent tags for real-time monitoring of gene expression. Secreted Alkaline Phosphatase (SEAP) kits assess extracellular enzyme activity. Chloramphenicol Acetyltransferase (CAT) and Beta-galactosidase (LacZ) kits measure enzyme activity related to specific gene regulation. Beta-glucuronidase (GUS) assay kits serve similar purposes in plant studies, while other assay kits cover diverse reporting needs.</p></p>
