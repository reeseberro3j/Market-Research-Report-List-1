<p><p>Methylene Diphenyl Isocyanate (MDI) is a crucial industrial chemical primarily used in the production of polyurethane foams. The market for MDI is divided into two main types: Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI. Pure MDI is characterized by its high reactivity and is primarily used in rigid foam applications, adhesives, and coatings. Polymeric MDI, on the other hand, consists of a mixture of polymetric isocyanates and is commonly employed in flexible foams, elastomers, and coatings, offering better processing characteristics and versatility.</p></p>
