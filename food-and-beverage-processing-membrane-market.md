<p><p>The Food and Beverage Processing Membrane Market is primarily divided into two types: polymeric membranes and ceramic membranes. Polymeric membranes are typically made from organic materials, offering cost-effectiveness and flexibility for applications like filtration and separation. They are widely used for processes such as water purification and juice clarification. Ceramic membranes, on the other hand, are made from inorganic materials, providing greater durability and resistance to high temperatures and harsh chemicals, making them suitable for sterile filtration and demanding environments.</p></p>
