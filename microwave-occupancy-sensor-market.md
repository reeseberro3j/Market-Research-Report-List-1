<p><p>The microwave occupancy sensor market is segmented into ceiling-mounted and wall-mounted types. Ceiling-mounted sensors are designed for overhead installation, offering wide coverage and ideal for larger spaces like warehouses and conference rooms. They emit microwave signals to detect motion within the designated area. Wall-mounted sensors are installed at eye level, making them suitable for smaller rooms and corridors. Both types enhance energy efficiency by automatically adjusting lighting and HVAC systems based on occupancy, promoting sustainable building practices.</p></p>
