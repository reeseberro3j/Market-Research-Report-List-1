<p><p>The parking meter device market is primarily divided into two types: single-space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single-space meters are designed to serve individual parking spots, allowing users to pay for their specific space, often with features like coin, card, or mobile payment options. In contrast, multi-space parking kiosks serve multiple parking spots, enabling drivers to pay at a central location and receive a ticket for display. Both types enhance parking efficiency and revenue collection for municipalities.</p></p>
