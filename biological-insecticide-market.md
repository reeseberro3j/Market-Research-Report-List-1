<p><p>The biological insecticide market encompasses three main types: microbial pesticides, plant pesticides, and biochemical pesticides. Microbial pesticides utilize natural organisms, such as bacteria or fungi, to target pests, offering a specific approach to pest control. Plant pesticides, derived from plant extracts, harness natural compounds to deter insects. Biochemical pesticides employ naturally occurring substances to disrupt pest development or behavior, promoting eco-friendly pest management. Together, these categories provide sustainable alternatives to chemical pesticides, addressing environmental and health concerns.</p></p>
