<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial cameras in the medical and life sciences market are primarily categorized into area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed images of stationary subjects, such as specimens in pathology. Line scan cameras capture one line at a time and are particularly useful for inspecting moving objects or surfaces, enhancing efficiency in quality control processes. Both types play critical roles in diagnostics, research, and automation.</p></p>
