<p><p>Hemodialysis water treatment plants utilize various technologies to ensure the purity of water used in dialysis. Carbon filtration removes chlorine and organic contaminants, while reverse osmosis effectively eliminates impurities. Ultrafiltration separates particles and microorganisms. Deionized water systems eliminate ionic contaminants. Ultraviolet TOC reduction addresses total organic carbon, enhancing water quality, while ultraviolet disinfection systems further sanitize water by inactivating pathogens. The single patient (acute) treatments market focuses on personalized water treatment solutions for individual patients undergoing urgent dialysis, ensuring their safety and health.</p></p>
