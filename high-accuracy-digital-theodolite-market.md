<p><p>The high accuracy digital theodolite market primarily comprises two types: optical theodolites and electronic theodolites. Optical theodolites utilize lenses and optical systems for precise angle measurement, relying on manual sighting. In contrast, electronic theodolites incorporate digital displays and electronic sensors, enhancing accuracy and ease of use with functionalities like automatic angle tracking and data storage. Both types are essential in surveying and construction, offering distinct advantages depending on the application's technical requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
