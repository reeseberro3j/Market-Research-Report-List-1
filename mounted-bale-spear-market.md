<p><p>The mounted bale spear market includes three main types: single-fork, double-fork, and multi-fork spears. Single-fork models are ideal for handling smaller bales, offering simplicity and maneuverability. Double-fork spears provide added stability and are better suited for larger bales, allowing for more efficient lifting and transport. Multi-fork spears, featuring several prongs, cater to operations requiring maximum load capacity and versatility, enabling users to manage multiple bales simultaneously. Each type serves different operational needs in agriculture and material handling.</p></p>
