<p><strong>The Stirling Cryocoolers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Cryocoolers</li><li>Rotary Cryocoolers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stirling cryocoolers market can be categorized into linear and rotary cryocoolers. Linear cryocoolers utilize a reciprocating mechanism to compress and expand gas, offering high efficiency and reliability, making them ideal for applications like infrared detectors and medical devices. Rotary cryocoolers, on the other hand, employ a rotating mechanism for gas movement, providing compact design and lower vibration, suitable for applications in space exploration and aerospace. Both types cater to diverse cooling needs across various industries.</p></p>
